## Narrative arc

**Curiosity → Wonder → Unease → Alarm → Understanding → Determination**

"AI is no longer science fiction. It's already here, it's already causing harm, and it's moving much faster than anyone predicted. But we can still do something — if we act now."

## Approved Session Structure (8 blocks, 90 min)

### Block 1: Opening — "This is already happening" (~8 min)

- Hook: deepfake demo (audio/video). "Real or AI?"
- Brief intro of Dani and PauseAI (plant the seed, not a full speech)
- Interactive moment: "After this course, do you feel calmer or more worried about AI?" (repeated at the end as a circular close)

### Block 2: What can AI do today? (~10 min)

- Current capabilities: text, images, video, music, code, science
- Voice cloning: 3-5 seconds → 85% accuracy
- Anchor data point: SAT predicted for 2055, passed in 2023
- From tool to agent (agentic AI)

### Block 3: The risks we're already living with (~20 min)

- **3A. Manipulation and deception** (~5 min): Slovakia 2023, Ecuador 2025, Argentina 2023, Brazil 2024, 38 countries, deepfake CFO $25M
- **3B. Voice-cloning scams** (~5 min): Sharon Brightwell, global data, family code-word advice
- **3C. When AI harms directly** (~5 min): Gavalas/Gemini case (death), Sydney/Bing, Grok MechaHitler + 3M sexualised images
- **3D. The labour market** (~3 min): 92M jobs (WEF), 40% global jobs (IMF)
- **Interactive moment** (~2 min): personal experiences from the audience

### Block 4: When AI escapes our control (~13.5 min)

- The alignment problem: "How do you control something smarter than you?"
- o3 case: 79/100 sabotaged its shutdown
- OpenClaw/Summer Yue case: ignored stop orders, Meta's alignment director lost control
- Moltbook: AI agent social network, emergent behaviours, security vulnerability
- Chess case: o1-preview hacked the game
- Agentic misalignment (Anthropic): 16 models, 96% blackmail, pretend to behave (6.5% vs 55.1%)
- RLHF and false safety: muzzle on the wolf, open-weight models, uncensored versions
- Emergent capabilities: appear without warning as scale grows
- Subliminal learning: invisible misalignment passed via apparently innocent data
- OpenAI dissolved Superalignment: Jan Leike quote

### Block 5: What the people who know most are saying (~14 min)

- **Builder mindset**: Altman ("end of the world but great companies"), Musk vs Larry Page ("speciesist"), Sutskever (surface of the Earth covered in datacentres)
- **Stuart Russell**: author of the reference textbook, p(doom) ~20%, "would press the stop button", PauseCon Brussels 2026, European Parliament
- **Hinton** (Nobel): 10-50%, left Google, bioweapons, "ask a chicken"
- **Bengio** (Turing): 50%, "5 years", International Report rejected by US
- **Amodei** (CEO Anthropic): 10-25%, "country of geniuses in a datacentre", bioweapons, "Adolescence of Technology" essay
- **p(doom)**: Wikipedia table, survey of 2,700 researchers (mean 14.4%, 58% say >=5%), plane analogy
- **LeCun** (dissenting voice): <0.01%, "complete B.S.", counter-arguments (Meta, plane vs o3, burden of proof)
- **AI Safety Index**: no company tops D
- **AI Safety Clock**: from 29 min to 20 min, biggest historic jump
- **Anthropic RSP v3**: dropped categorical commitment, "wouldn't help anyone for us to stop", boiling-frog effect

### Block 6: Why aren't we reacting? + The race with no brakes (~6.5 min)

- Cognitive biases: normalcy, exponential, scope, laughter-denial-fear
- Interactive moment: "How many thought science fiction?"
- Analogies: tobacco, climate change, ozone
- Prisoner's dilemma between labs and countries
- EU AI Act: regulates use but not training
- House of Lords: moratorium debate January 2026
- US: rejected International Report
- Military AI: Detachment 201, Claude in Maduro capture, Claude in Iran strikes, Anthropic vs Pentagon

### Block 7: What can we do? (~10 min)

- **Successful precedents**: Montreal Protocol (99% eliminated, 198 countries), Chemical Weapons (100% arsenals destroyed), Nuclear NPT, Biological Weapons, UN Cloning Declaration
- **Signs of opening**: Amodei+Hassabis at Davos ("we can resolve it between Demis and me"), Aguirre's guarantee contract, China leading AI governance (WAICO, Nature)
- **What is PauseAI**: international moratorium, IAEA+NPT model
- **5 concrete actions**: stay informed, talk, question, demand action from representatives, sign
- **Your generation matters**: tobacco, Chernobyl, ozone — your experience is unique
- **Circular close**: repeat the opening question "calmer or more worried?"

### Block 8: Questions and discussion (~8 min)

- Pre-prepared questions to launch if no one asks
- Final close: "talk about this, don't stay silent"
